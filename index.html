Atlas observes the global engine

The architecture of global capital is undeniable. Economic stability of the world (especially Australia) remains fundamentally subordinate to deviations in the US economy. Our purpose is to track data and stay ahead of the unknown.

Current vectors indicate exceptional economic risk. The conventional instruments for economic forecast (e.g. reliable US data sets) are declining in reliability and accuracy. Global markets are vulnerable to system ambiguity and exposed to sudden and severe fluctuations.

A current tension is the threat of rising unemployment, persistent inflation and stagnate productivity resulting in stagflation. If this tension were to pivot to structural recession then the resultant risk transfer to the global economy will be immediate and will impact everything from commodities and equities to capital flows. The Australian economy will experience direct, amplified pressure.

Atlas provides the critical objective data position to inform intelligent actions when the traditional signals fail. The Atlas Composite Score is a simple, objective measure of systemic risk we use to quantify threat.

We track Macro and Micro market indicators. Each indicator is checked against its own pre-defined stress thresholds and assigned an individual risk score. The system then sums all individual scores to create the final Composite Score. The final score maps directly to one of four risk postures to guide your portfolio's tactical defense.
