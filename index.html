Atlas observes the global engine

The architecture of global capital is undeniable. Economic stability of the world (especially Australia) remains fundamentally subordinate to deviations in the US economy. Our purpose is to track data and stay ahead of the unknown.

Current vectors indicate exceptional economic risk. The conventional instruments for economic forecast (e.g. reliable US data sets) are declining in reliability and accuracy. Global markets are vulnerable to system ambiguity and exposed to sudden and severe fluctuations.

A current tension is the mounting threat of rising unemployment, persistent inflation and stagnate productivity resulting in the corrosive stagflation. If this tension were to pivot to structural recession then the resultant risk transfer to the global economy will be immediate and will impact everything from commodities and equities to capital flows. The Australian economy will experience direct, amplified pressure.

Atlas provides the critical objective data position to inform intelligent actions when the traditional signals fail.
